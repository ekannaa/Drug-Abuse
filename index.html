Effects Of Drug Abuse.

Drug abuse is a very dangerous activity that is associated with several devatating repercussions. People accustomed to drugs suffer numerous side effects ranging from psychological to social consequences. In terms of health,drug abuse leads to psychologicical disorders.Newcomb,Scheier,and Bentler posit,"Mental health problems are often observed in among drug abusers" (215).The psychological impacts of drug abuse can appear immediately or even later in the abuser's life.
Clinically examined there is a great relationship between drug abuse and psychopathology.

People with addiction often have one or more associated health issues,
which could include lung or heart disease,stroke,cancer, or mental health conditions .
Imaging scans,chest X-rays,and blood tests can show the damaging effects of long-term drug use throughout the body.
